    Featured Drink

    Ingredients:

    1. 2 ounces light rum
    2. 1 1/2 ounces cream of coconut
    3. 1 1/2 ounces pineapple juice
    4. 1/2 ounce lime juice, freshly squeezed
    5. Garnish: pineapple wedge
    6. Garnish: pineapple leaf

    Directions:

    1. Add the rum, cream of coconut and pineapple and lime juices
      to a shaker with ice and shake vigorously for 20 to 30 seconds.
    2. Strain into a chilled Hurricane glass over pebble ice.
    3. Garnish with a pineapple wedge and pineapple leaf.
